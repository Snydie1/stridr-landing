Stridr — SMS opt-in flow (Call to Action)

This page documents how a consumer opts in to Stridr's accountability ("Backup") text messages.

  1. A Stridr app user invites one trusted friend to be their accountability Backup, confirming they have that person's permission to be contacted.
  2. Stridr sends the invited friend a single opt-in invitation SMS: "Stridr: Alex added you as their accountability Backup. We only text if they go quiet for 3+ days. Reply YES to confirm, STOP to opt out, HELP for help. Msg & data rates may apply."
  3. Recurring messages begin only after the recipient replies YES (double opt-in). The recipient may also use the web-based consent page shown below, reached via the personal invite link, which requires ticking an explicit consent checkbox before confirming.
  4. Reply STOP at any time to opt out; HELP for help. Message frequency: maximum 1 message per week. Msg & data rates may apply.
