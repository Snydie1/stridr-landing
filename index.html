How it works

Your goal, your voice.

Five coaching voices, three intensities — from gentle to drill sergeant. You pick how Stridr talks to you.

The evening check-in.

Score the day honestly. Streaks with real rules: grace days you earn, comebacks that repair, rest mode when life happens.

A Backup in your corner.

Optionally pick one person who gets a single text if you ever go quiet — never more than one a week, no app needed.

Text messaging

Stridr sends text messages only to users and to Backup contacts who opt in via a consent page. Message frequency varies (typically no more than one message per week for Backup contacts). Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help. See our Terms and Privacy Policy.
